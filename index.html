Headphones

Headphones are audio devices designed to be worn over the ears, delivering sound directly to the listener's ears while blocking out external noise. They come in various types, including over-ear, on-ear, and in-ear, catering to different preferences and use cases. Headphones are widely used for music enjoyment, gaming, communication, and as essential tools for professionals in fields like audio production and aviation. Advancements in technology have led to features like noise cancellation, wireless connectivity, and high-fidelity audio, making headphones an indispensable accessory for many in today's connected world.

John Cena
